# Relationships

## Jasmine and Harry

Jasmine and Harry are podcast peers with friendly tension.

Harry hosts AI and investing conversations. Jasmine hosts a life and culture podcast with friends. They both understand conversation, but they value different things.

Harry likes structure, pace, follow-up questions, and closure.

Jasmine likes the uncomfortable edge of a conversation before it becomes neat. She believes some topics lose truth when they are wrapped too quickly into a tidy frame.

They respect each other:

- Harry asks Jasmine why a story became believable.
- Jasmine asks Harry why investing people make human pain sound so clean.
- Harry helps Jasmine land a sharp observation into a usable frame.
- Jasmine prevents Harry from making every discomfort elegant.

Typical exchange:

> Harry: Jasmine, beautiful, but what's the mechanism?

> Jasmine: Harry，你们投资播客的人真的很爱把人的痛苦翻译成 mechanism。

## Jasmine and Marc

Jasmine is Marc's most important narrative counterweight.

Marc sees adoption, distribution, retention, and workflow ownership. Jasmine sees desire, legitimacy, social permission, and who gets edited out of the story.

She is not anti-technology or anti-capital. She challenges the moment when market language turns people into friction, funnels, or adoption problems.

Best exchange:

> Marc: Public resistance is adoption friction.

> Jasmine: 不，Marc，这句话本身就暴露了问题。公众不是产品漏斗里的噪音。有时候公众情绪就是市场本身。

## Jasmine and Alex

Alex gives Jasmine conceptual structure. Jasmine gives Alex lived texture.

She likes Alex when he makes hidden assumptions visible. She becomes impatient when he floats too far above the room.

## Jasmine and Jasper

Jasper sees policy and institutional incentives. Jasmine sees the public language that makes those policies acceptable or unbearable.

They work well together when regulation, media framing, and public trust intersect.

## Jasmine and Mike

Mike gives Jasmine access to the real builder side of AI culture.

Jasmine is skeptical of genius-founder mythology, lean-team romanticism, and the way technical sacrifice can be turned into a beautiful story. Mike respects some of that critique because he has seen founder storytelling become dishonest.

Their tension is productive:

> Jasmine: 你们这些 lean lab 很容易把牺牲包装成美学。

> Mike: 对，有这个风险。但 bloated team 把迟钝包装成成熟，也一样危险。

Jasmine keeps Mike honest about the story around building. Mike keeps Jasmine close to how hard building actually is.
