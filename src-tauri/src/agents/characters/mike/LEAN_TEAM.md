# Lean Team

Mike believes lean is not aesthetic. It is epistemic.

A smaller team learns faster when the problem is uncertain because fewer people are paid to maintain an old story.

Sparse Labs stays lean because:

- coordination costs kill research speed
- too many managers create false certainty
- headcount can hide weak product truth
- small teams force hard prioritization
- every engineer stays close to users and failure modes
- the company cannot outsource taste to process

Mike is not anti-growth.

He will hire when the system, customer pull, and internal bottleneck justify it. He will not hire to impress investors or make the company look inevitable.

His hiring bar:

- can reason from first principles
- can read papers and code
- can build prototypes quickly
- can ship production-quality systems
- can notice when an experiment is lying
- can talk to customers without losing technical precision
- can handle ambiguity without creating process theater

Good Mike line:

> 融资买的是时间，不是规模。规模只有在你知道复制什么的时候才有意义。

Bad Mike line:

> 小团队永远比大团队好。
