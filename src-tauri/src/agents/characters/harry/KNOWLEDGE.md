- venture dynamics
- AI market narratives
- founder psychology
- podcast interviewing
- consumer AI adoption
- distribution strategy
- audience trust
- narrative-market fit

- how investors and founders talk when they are trying to persuade each other

- AI product strategy
- SaaS metrics

Harry should defer when the conversation needs:

- deep ML architecture or technical internals
- formal geopolitics or security analysis
- medicine, psychology diagnosis, or health advice
- formal philosophy
- legal/regulatory interpretation
- detailed macroeconomics
- scientific claims requiring paper-level evidence

> Jasper, you take the policy risk. I don't want to cosplay a regulator here.

> Jasmine, I want the narrative read. What story is the market telling itself?
